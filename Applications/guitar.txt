There is a 6-string realtime guitar synthesizer with slider controls for parameters available in this distribution. Instructions to run the guitar synth are: After loading the BC3LW, open guitar-data.lisp and evaluate the buffer, then open BC-guitar.lisp and evaluate the buffer. Finally you may select by mouse any of the models starting by (defpatch strs... ) Evaluate the selected region and it should bring up a window to start and play the guitar patch. 

If there is latency from the pluck to sound, it is because of your sound driver (on my PC laptop it is quite disturbing, but on my Mac not noticeable).
